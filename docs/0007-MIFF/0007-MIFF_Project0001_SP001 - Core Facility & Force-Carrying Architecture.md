## Phase 1 — Functional Zoning (HFZ / IAZ)  
*(Violence and calm must never mix)*

### Scope
- High-Force Zones (HFZ)
- Intermediate Absorption Zones (IAZ)
- Precision-adjacent buffers

### Doctrine
- HFZ and IAZ are physically separated
- **HFZ vibration spectra must be measured and logged, not assumed**
- Zones are enforced by mass, distance, and damping — not signage

**Estimated R&D Cost:** $200k – $600k  

**R&D Value:**  
Force ignored will travel.
