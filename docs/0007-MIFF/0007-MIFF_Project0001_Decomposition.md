## What MIFF Project0001 Is

MIFF Project0001 is a **complete industrial lifecycle system** capable of:

- moving mass safely  
- shaping matter responsibly  
- assembling large structures truthfully  
- powering systems quietly and reliably  
- coordinating humans and machines  
- validating strength against reality  
- surviving transport and field deployment  
- aging, repairing, and exiting cleanly  

It is not a factory.  
It is a **capability stack**.

---

## Decomposition Philosophy

MIFF Project0001 is decomposed along **irreducible failure boundaries**.

Each SP exists because:
- combining it with another domain would hide failure
- separating it makes responsibility explicit
- readiness can be asserted without bluffing

> **If a failure can occur independently, it deserves its own SP.**

## High-Level Capability Stack

MIFF Project0001 decomposes into **eight sequential capability layers**:

1. **Movement**
2. **Fabrication**
3. **Assembly & Geometric Truth**
4. **Utilities & Life Support**
5. **Automation & Coordination**
6. **Validation & Proof**
7. **Field Reality**
8. **Time, Care & Return**

Each layer builds on the previous.  
No layer retroactively fixes a missing one.

## SP Decomposition Map

### SP003 — Lifting, Handling & Internal Logistics  
**Capability:** Safe movement of mass  
**Failure Mode Isolated:** Accidental force, momentum, human exposure  

- Defines how mass moves
- Removes humans from load paths
- Creates predictable internal flow

> Without SP003, nothing else is safe.

---

### SP004 — Heavy Fabrication & Process Zoning  
**Capability:** Shaping matter without exporting chaos  
**Failure Mode Isolated:** Fire, dust, vibration, contamination  

- Separates hot, loud, dirty from precision
- Enables subtractive, forming, additive work
- Contains sparks, heat, and noise

> Without SP004, precision and safety collapse.

---

### SP005 — Structural Assembly, Fixturing & Alignment  
**Capability:** Geometric truth at scale  
**Failure Mode Isolated:** Warp, weld pull, thermal drift  

- Defines datums and alignment ownership
- Ensures assemblies are square before strong
- Locks geometry before joining

> Without SP005, strength is an illusion.

---

### SP006 — Power, Utilities & Industrial Services  
**Capability:** Quiet, stable life support  
**Failure Mode Isolated:** Cascading failures, drift, downtime  

- Delivers power, air, water, cooling
- Isolates dirty services from clean zones
- Localizes failures instantly

> Without SP006, everything dies unpredictably.

---

### SP007 — Automation, Heavy Robotics & Assisted Fabrication  
**Capability:** Coordinated force multiplication  
**Failure Mode Isolated:** Automation runaway, choreography errors  

- Assists humans without replacing judgment
- Constrains robots deliberately
- Enforces safe halts and coordination logic

> Without SP007, scale becomes dangerous.

---

### SP008 — Quality Assurance, Testing & Structural Validation  
**Capability:** Proof against reality  
**Failure Mode Isolated:** Unknown strength, silent degradation  

- Measures load, fatigue, failure
- Captures data instead of opinions
- Enables certification-grade truth

> Without SP008, confidence is unearned.

---

### SP009 — Assembly, Transport & Field Deployment Validation  
**Capability:** Survival outside the lab  
**Failure Mode Isolated:** Shipping, weather, human error  

- Validates lifting, transport, reassembly
- Tests environmental exposure
- Closes the lab-to-field gap

> Without SP009, designs are fragile.

---

### SP010 — Lifecycle, Maintenance & Decommissioning  
**Capability:** Dignified time horizon  
**Failure Mode Isolated:** Silent decay, abandonment  

- Makes degradation visible
- Enables repair without heroics
- Plans clean exit and material return

> Without SP010, infrastructure becomes a liability.

## What This Decomposition Explicitly Rejects

MIFF Project0001 does **not** rely on:

- hero operators  
- undocumented tribal knowledge  
- optimistic assumptions  
- deferred safety  
- “we’ll fix it later” debt  

If a capability is required, it is named.  
If a risk exists, it is isolated.
