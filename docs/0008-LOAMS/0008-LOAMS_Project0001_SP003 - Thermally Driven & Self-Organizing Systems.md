## Thermally Driven & Self-Organizing Systems  
*Entropy as Signal · Heat as Architect · Structure Without Command*

---

## R&D Framing (Non-Negotiable)

SP003 investigates how **gradients organize matter**.

The goal is not to dominate heat, extract power, or induce uncontrolled reactions,  
but to **observe how structure emerges when physical laws are allowed to act**.

This SP studies:
- thermal gradients as organizing logic
- phase transitions as information carriers
- form emerging without centralized control

This SP explicitly excludes:
- power generation
- weapons research
- runaway or uncontained reactions :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Phase 1 — Thermal Gradient Generation & Control

### Pass-1 Refinement
Only **low-energy, controllable** thermal regimes are permitted:

- Precision heat sources (bounded power)
- Gradient ramp / hold controllers
- Insulated test chambers
- Independent emergency thermal cutoffs

**Correctness constraint:**  
> If the gradient cannot be held and reversed, escalation is forbidden.

**Cost:** ~$5k–$40k  
**R&D Value:** Without gradient control, nothing else is interpretable. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

### Pass-1 Refinement

- Modular containment vessels
- Thermal and pressure isolation layers
- Observation ports (optical + sensor)
- Physical kill-switches
- Automated shutdown logic

**Rule:**  
> No uncontrolled transitions. Ever.

**Cost:** ~$5k–$35k  
**R&D Value:** Curiosity must remain inside walls. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
